Why GrooveLinx exists
The friction points

Bands stall for predictable reasons

  • One person isn’t prepared → everyone suffers.
  • Parts step on each other → the music gets muddy and stops breathing.
  • “What song do you want to do?” → rehearsal time gets burned on indecision.
  • Creative breakthroughs get lost → no simple place to capture notes, fixes, reminders.
  • Scheduling 5 lives is brutal → availability ping-pong kills momentum.
  • No shared definition of “the bomb” → nobody aligns on what “ready” means.
The GrooveLinx answer

A command center built for jam bands

Go deep when you want to (charts, structure, notes, recordings)… or flip into simplified overlays: Practice Mode, Rehearsal Mode, and Gig Mode with the essentials at your fingertips.

  • North Star: define “the bomb” and track readiness.
  • Practice tracker: small chunks, visible progress, less overwhelm.
  • Capture + remember: notes + recordings tied to the exact section.
  • Availability: one place to coordinate rehearsals and gigs.
  • Cover Me: inspiration library so everyone levels up together.
  • One source of truth: every version of a song + curated playlists/setlists.
What it does

50% psychedelic soul. 50% modern product polish.

The vibe matters — but so does clarity. GrooveLinx keeps the art up front while quietly removing the junk that slows bands down.

Song Library (one source of truth)

Keys, BPM, structure, charts, notes, recordings — searchable and filterable.

Charts + Structure (stage-ready)

Clean, readable charts with your exact arrangement (no more guessing which version).

North Star readiness

Agree on what “the bomb” looks like, then track progress toward it.

Best Shot vs North Star

Rate sections, capture the weak spots, and put the next rep where it matters.

Rehearsal Chopper

Split messy rehearsal recordings into usable takes — save clean clips fast.

Notes that actually stick

Capture creative moments and recurring mistakes so you don’t re-learn the same lesson.

Cover Me inspiration

Learn from other musicians’ approaches — a built-in “how others do it” library.

Playlists + setlists

Curate every version of a song and build playlists in any order — always at hand.

Availability + scheduling

Get everyone’s availability in one place. Stop the group-text scheduling spiral.
