Why GrooveLinx exists

Bands stall for predictable reasons

Jam bands are unforgiving: if one person isn’t practiced, locked in, and listening, the whole band feels it. GrooveLinx makes “ready” visible, keeps priorities clear, and captures the magic before it evaporates.
