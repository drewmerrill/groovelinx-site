Learn songs together, not alone.

Practice-first workflows that make your band tighter — faster.

  • Song DNA: key, tempo, structure, roles, harmonies, and notes.
  • North Star: band-voted reference version (Archive / YouTube / Spotify).
  • Best Shot: your current best take — compare and improve.
  • Woodshed: practice tracks, stems, harmony parts, and rehearsal notes.
Practice workflow, dialed:
Pick the North Star version
Extract stems and loop sections
Rehearse → capture Best Shot
Rate sections + track progress

Keep the whole band in sync.
