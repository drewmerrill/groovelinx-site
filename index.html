Where bands lock in.
Less admin. More jams.

GrooveLinx removes the friction that keeps bands from reaching their full potential: practice accountability, rehearsal flow, capturing ideas, and scheduling — without killing the vibe.

  • No more “what do you want to play?” Walk in with a plan, focus on the hard parts, and get better faster.
  • Define what “the bomb” looks like. Align on a North Star reference and rate each section until you’re truly locked in.
  • Capture the magic. Notes + rehearsal recordings + reminders so creative moments don’t evaporate.
  • One place for availability & gigs. Stop the 40-text scheduling thread. Just sync and go.
