Built for the Jam Band Community

GrooveLinx is for bands that live in the space between the notes—where listening, trust, and musical risk-taking matter more than backing tracks.

Whether you're covering Grateful Dead, Phish, Goose, Widespread Panic, Allman Brothers, or building your own jam vehicle, GrooveLinx helps your band stay organized, rehearse smarter, and lock into the groove together.

From basement jams to festival stages, GrooveLinx keeps everyone on the same page so the music can wander wherever it wants.

Learn songs together, not alone.
Everything from your current app, tuned for any jam band.
  • Song Library & Song DNA: one place for key, BPM, lead singer, harmonies, structure, and notes.
  • The North Star: band-voted reference version (Archive.org, YouTube, Spotify, etc.).
  • Best Shot vs North Star: compare your live take to the reference and track progress by section.
  • Stage Crib Notes: charts and lyrics ready for iPad / stage use.
  • The Woodshed: practice tracks, harmony parts, Moises stems, and rehearsal notes in one view.
Practice workflow, dialed:
Pull shows from Archive.org or YouTube
Upload to Moises & separate stems
Loop & slow down tricky sections
Share practice tracks with the whole band
Keep the whole band in sync.
Not just songs—the boring-but-essential stuff too.
  • Shared calendar: rehearsals, gigs, and important dates.
  • Gig logistics: venues, contacts, pay, load-in and set times.
  • Playlists & setlists: first set, second set, encores, and segues planned in advance.
  • Utilities: built-in tuner and metronome for quick checks.
