The friction GrooveLinx removes

  • One person unprepared → the whole band suffers.
  • Stepping on parts → the mix gets muddy and the music can’t breathe.
  • “What song do you want to do?” → wasted rehearsal time, slow growth.
  • Creative moments vanish because there’s no quick way to capture notes.
  • Practice feels insurmountable without bite-sized tracking and progress signals.
  • Scheduling five lives is painful without one shared availability view.
